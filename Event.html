Our Events

Something short and leading about the collection of our program and Events. We truly believe that to achieve success & to make a difference we need to utilize the existing resources & make each and every animal lover a part of the NGO.

World Animal Day Celebration

World Animal Day is also known as the Animal Lovers Day as it encourages love, care and protection of animals through the actions of individuals and organizations that work for the welfare of domestic and wild animals.

World Rabies Day

The occasion of the World Rabies Day, on the 28th of September 2020. The topic was "The Importance of Collaboration to Contribute to the Welfare of Stray Dogs and End Rabies" . We vaccinate and sterilize stray dogs so that people can live with out fear of Rabies.

Caring for abandoned equines during lockdown

Animals has been supplementing food for street animals suffering due to 2020 Covid lockdown. We have been receiving a regular intake of severely malnourished donkeys & horsesat our shelter and taking care for them.

First Aid Workshop

Save life in association with Students will be conducting a workshop on First Aid for Dogs and Cats on the campus. The students will learn various methods to help animals in distress and in many cases, even go to the extent of saving a life.

Animal Stories Marathon

Save life Foundation Conducting a Animal Stories Marathon. Where Students Can Apply and Telling the Stories About Animals. These types of Marathon conduct for to Awaring people about animals.


Save Animals from Plastic

Save life was Conduct a program to save Animals from plastic. Plastic is very harmful for animals.When animals ingest plastic waste, it can block their digestive tracts. As a result, they starve. Toxic chemicals in plastic can harm animals' health.

Animal Abuse and Rescue

Those animals who are abused by people and they does not safe in the streets. To save their life we planed a Compaign. and Gave them a place at Our shelter. We are providing a immediate relief to animals via a thorough medical check-up. and It is a legal offense to harass animals.


Community Cat Sterilization, Care, & Shelter

Save life Foundation support cat sterilization projects, feline veterinary training, cat rescues, and cat shelters to worthwhile organizations. Our feline projects have ranged from rescuing cats from the horrific cat meat trade to building feline shelters to TNR (trap-neuter-release) community cats.

Saving Camels from Slaughter and Abuse

Captive camels in India have a hard life as they are used and abused for labor , milk and are slaughtered for their meat. We Gave them a place at Our shelter and provided with immediate relief via a thorough medical check-up.
